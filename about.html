About Us

Our company would like to make a one stop shop for the people who are trying to be independent. We bring meals if the client wants meals already prepared or help them prepare their own meals. We perform housework chores that need to be done. We help with client’s personal care and tend to any of the homecare needs as well. If you need a ride to an appointment or to a store or just want to get out of the house we can do that for you as well. Our company will offer Registered / certified people (nurse or psw) to assist in accomplishing everyday tasks to help people stay in their homes and live independently.

Our company helps with everything from medical care, showers, house cleaning to bringing the client to appointments or even out to shop. As an attendant care service, our clients would reserve blocks of time with our certified people with pricing based on qualifications required. This would allow them to list whatever services they required that day, whether it is cleaning and care or just driving them to an appointment. Even if it is just someone to talk to or eat lunch with our services will cover anything they require. We will partner with trusted, bonded companies to even offer the overseeing of maintenance with their properties. Each and every client and their home will be our first priority.



Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to build a partnership with our clients and make them safe at home. Whether you just need a person to take you out to the store, or with a shower. We are here for you. It will be the duty of every single employee to respect the client's wishes. No problem is too small or large for our company. We will be there not only to help with the client, but to take as much of the burden off of the families to alleviate the stress and time that is usually placed upon them. With our registered staff, we will be able to constantly monitor the client to be sure they are safe in their homes, properly nourished, medically cared for and even help with loneliness.

Our Vision Statement

As a company, we would like people to stay as independent as possible. We are here to help you achieve your goals while being independent at home. Allowing you and your family to enjoy a worry free lifestyle, while being cared for. As a company dedicated to the clients needs, we hope to allow people to not only stay in their homes independently for longer, but help to take some burden off our ailing health care system. People allowed to live their life in familiar surroundings, will feel more dignified in their daily lives. Our vision is to see all of our clients happy and healthy living as independent as possible in their own surroundings.
