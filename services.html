House Work

No job is too small or too large, our staff is trained to accomplish any task you require. Included but not limited to, sweeping floors, vacuuming floors/stairs, washing baseboards or moldings, washing dishes, washing windows, washing floors, dusting, laundry, cleaning stove, and cleaning fridge. Consider our team your personal assistant for the time you book.

$25/hour

Medical Care

All procedures performed by a home care service. Included but not limited to, wound care, medication administration, physio therapy, oxygen therapy, colostomy care, and bathing. We also have Physio Therapists on staff for in home physio therapy programs and massage.

Prices vary, please call for pricing.

Need a Shopping Buddy?

If you want to just get out for the day and go shopping we will be your shopping buddy. We will pick you up and take you wherever you want to go. Not only do we provide the vehicle ride, but the companionship as well.

$25/hour, for special mobility vehicle extra charges will apply.
